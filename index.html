What is the American Natational Park Serviece?

 The US National Parks are an amazing resource for learning more about our land's rich history and people, and or just a place to get away from it all and enjoy the beautiful landscape. People have been enjoying the parks since 1872 when yellowstone was made into the first national park, after the national parks act was signed. The NPS is a federal institution assigned with the protection and preservation of our countries designated National Monuments, Memorials, Historic Sights, and its unique and grand National Parks. Why we need the National Parks

 The National Parks are playing a vital role in conservation of endangered animals and threatened ecosystems. Humans have done a perfect job at destroying the environment. To list an example, in the late 1800's buffalo were almost hunted to extinction in order to choke out native american populations. Settlers killed millions of buffalo in their effort to build a coast to coast railroad system. This massive hunt led to disturbing photos like the one to the right -- all buffalo skulls. At Yellowstone National Park, both the decimated populations of wild Buffalo and Grey Wolf have been renewed. We need national parks to ensure the safety of species like these and other endangered animals.
